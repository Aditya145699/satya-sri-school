Classes We Offer

Our School offers Class 1 to Class 12 with a great environment and
well trained, experienced teacher.

Class 1 to 5

our primary level curriculum lays the foundation for lifelong learning. Beginning with interactive play-based learning in Class 1, students progress through hands-on activities and collaborative projects in subsequent years. Each class focuses on building essential skills such as literacy, numeracy, critical thinking, and problem-solving, preparing students for a successful transition to upper primary education.

Class 6 to 8

Classes 6 to 8 mark the upper primary years, where students delve deeper into academic subjects while continuing to develop essential skills for future success. With a focus on critical thinking, problem-solving, and independent learning, our curriculum challenges students to explore complex topics and engage in interdisciplinary learning experiences. From science experiments to literary analysis, students are encouraged to question, investigate, and innovate. These years are pivotal in shaping students' academic and personal growth, preparing them for the challenges of higher education and beyond.

Class 9 to 12

Classes 9 to 12 encompass the high school years, where students delve deeper into specialized subjects and prepare for higher education or career pathways. With a focus on academic excellence, critical thinking, and personal development, our curriculum equips students with the knowledge, skills, and values necessary for success in an ever-changing world. Through rigorous coursework, experiential learning opportunities, and comprehensive assessments, students are empowered to pursue their passions, achieve their goals, and make meaningful contributions to society.
